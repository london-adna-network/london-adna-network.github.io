November 2024 Meeting - festive networking

To end our first year of the re-started London aDNA Network we held some festive drinks in Bloomsbury for aDNA researchers before the end of the academic term.

As a handful of early career researchers, we started the Network midway through this year after it had become dormant over COVID. What made this possible was our friends and colleagues supporting us. This event was a really nice way to discuss what our members want to do with the Network next year and celebrate all they have done this year!
